About


Biomedical Scientist & Bioinformatician.



I am currently a PhD candidate in Health Sciences, focusing on pathogenesis and virulence in CHIKV, DENV, and ZIKV infection.


I have experience with sequencing and analysis of NGS data: pre-processing, assembly, phylogenetic and analysis of RNA-Seq differential gene expression.

Nowadays, I'm working with molecular surveillance and diagnosis of emerging and re-emerging arboviruses and SARS-CoV-2.
